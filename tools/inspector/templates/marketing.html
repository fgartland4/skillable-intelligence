Marketing Batch Analysis

Score a list of companies at once. The engine analyzes each company's top product and produces a ranked table for ABM targeting — exportable to CSV for HubSpot.

How it works: For each company, the engine discovers their top product, researches it, and scores it automatically. One contact per company is identified. Results are ranked by composite opportunity score. Expect 3–6 minutes per company — run during off-peak hours for large lists.
One company per line (or comma-separated). Limit to 20 companies per batch for best results.
